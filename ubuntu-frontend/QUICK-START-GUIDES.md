# üöÄ Quick Start Guide for Tour Guides

## Welcome to Ubuntu Travels!

This guide will help you set up your profile and start appearing on the homepage in just a few minutes.

---

## Step 1: Register Your Account (2 minutes)

1. Go to: **http://localhost:8000/auth.html?role=guide**
2. Click the **"Tour Guide"** option
3. Click **"Register"** tab
4. Fill in the registration form:
   - First Name & Last Name
   - Email & Password
   - Phone Number
   - Country
   - Accept Terms & Conditions
5. Click **"Create Account"**

‚úÖ You're now registered! You'll see a message that your account needs admin approval.

---

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile (5 minutes)

1. After registration, you'll be redirected to your dashboard
2. Click the **"Edit Profile"** button (top right)
3. **Upload Your Photo:**
   - Click "Choose Photo"
   - Select a professional headshot
   - Wait for upload to complete
   
4. **Fill Required Fields:**
   - **Short Introduction** (200 chars) - This appears on homepage cards
     - Example: "Expert in gorilla trekking and wildlife safaris across Rwanda"
   - **Full Biography** (1000 chars) - This appears on your bio page
     - Tell your story, experience, and what makes you special
   - **Years of Experience** - How long you've been guiding
   
5. **Select Your Details:**
   - Check all **Languages** you speak
   - Check your **Tour Specializations**
   - Add **Company Name** (optional)
   - Select **Business Type** (optional)

6. Click **"Save Profile"**

‚úÖ Your profile is now complete!

---

## Step 3: Wait for Admin Approval (Varies)

1. An admin will review your application
2. You'll be notified when approved (check back on dashboard)
3. Once approved, you can create tours
4. Admin may also mark you as "verified" for a badge

---

## Step 4: Appear on Homepage (Automatic!)

Once you're approved and verified:
- ‚úÖ Your profile automatically appears on the homepage
- ‚úÖ Visitors see your photo and short intro
- ‚úÖ They can click "Read my Bio" to see your full profile
- ‚úÖ They can click "View Tours" to see your tours

**No manual work needed - it's all automatic!**

## ‚úçÔ∏è Writing Your Short Introduction

This is the MOST IMPORTANT text - it appears on the homepage!

### Good Examples ‚úÖ
- "Expert in gorilla trekking and wildlife safaris across Rwanda"
- "Cultural heritage specialist and mountain hiking expert"
- "Safari expert with deep knowledge of Maasai Mara ecosystem"
- "Kilimanjaro climbing specialist and Serengeti safari guide"

### Bad Examples ‚ùå
- "I am a tour guide" (too generic)
- "Contact me for tours" (not descriptive)
- "Best guide in Africa" (sounds fake)
- Long paragraphs (keep it to 1-2 sentences)

### Formula
**"[Specialty] + [Experience/Location]"**

Examples:
- "Wildlife photographer and safari guide in Tanzania"
- "Certified mountain guide specializing in Kilimanjaro treks"
- "Cultural tour expert with 15 years in Rwanda"

---

## üìù Writing Your Full Biography

This appears on your dedicated bio page. Make it personal and compelling!

### Structure
1. **Opening** - Who you are and what you do
2. **Experience** - How long, what you've done
3. **Passion** - Why you love guiding
4. **Specialties** - What makes you unique
5. **Call to Action** - Invite them to book

### Example
```
With over 10 years of experience guiding tourists through Rwanda's 
stunning landscapes, I specialize in gorilla trekking expeditions in 
Volcanoes National Park. 

My journey began as a park ranger, where I developed a deep 
understanding of Rwanda's wildlife and ecosystems. Today, I combine 
this knowledge with my passion for storytelling to create 
unforgettable experiences for visitors from around the world.

I speak English, French, and Kinyarwanda fluently, allowing me to 
connect with diverse groups of travelers. Whether you're seeking 
adventure, wildlife photography, or cultural immersion, I'll ensure 
your Rwanda experience exceeds expectations.

Let's explore the land of a thousand hills together!
```

## üÜò Common Issues

### "My profile isn't showing on homepage"
**Solution:** You need to be both approved AND verified by admin.

### "Image upload failed"
**Solutions:**
- Check file size (must be < 5MB)
- Check file type (must be image)
- Try a different image
- Check internet connection

### "Can't save profile"
**Solutions:**
- Fill all required fields (marked with *)
- Check character limits (short intro: 200, bio: 1000)
- Make sure you're logged in
- Try refreshing the page

### "Changes not appearing"
**Solution:** Hard refresh your browser (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac, Ctrl+Shift+R on Windows)
